<h1 style="color:#f8e8c7; font-size:2rem; margin-bottom:1rem;">
  Chronological Timeline of the Known World
</h1>

<p style="opacity:0.85; font-style:italic;">
A history of Atlantis and the Neokosmos region, compiled from surviving sources after the Age of Grinding Ice.  
Modern historians estimate the Fall of Atlantis to around 12,000 BC in present reckoning.
</p>


<!-- MYTHIC AGE -->
<h2 style="color:#f0dfc4; margin-top:2rem;">The Mythic Age</h2>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~6000 B.F. — The Waning of Ice</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> The last great glaciers recede as the world warms.</li>
  <li><strong>Significance:</strong> Seas rise, reshaping continents; forests spread across Neokosmos and the Western Reaches.</li>
  <li><strong>Result:</strong> The Age of Ice ends; early signs of civilization begin.</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~5000 B.F. — The Awakening of the Elves</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Elves awaken in the deep forests, an era remembered as the Elven Spring.</li>
  <li><strong>Culture:</strong> They bring music, art, and magic intertwined with leylines.</li>
  <li><strong>Impact:</strong> They become nature’s stewards and first masters of mana.</li>
</ul>


<!-- DAWN OF HUMANITY -->
<h2 style="color:#f0dfc4; margin-top:2rem;">The Dawn of Humanity</h2>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~3000 B.F. — The Age of the Watchers</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Humanity expands into central Neokosmos.</li>
  <li><strong>Elven Response:</strong> The Elven Watch is established—quiet guardianship and observation.</li>
  <li><strong>Outcome:</strong> Human cities rise under subtle elven influence.</li>
</ul>


<!-- AGE OF ATLANTIS -->
<h2 style="color:#f0dfc4; margin-top:2rem;">The Age of Atlantis</h2>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~2000 B.F. — Founding of Atlantis</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Seafaring humans found the Republic of Atlantis.</li>
  <li><strong>Character:</strong> A center of art, science, and trade.</li>
  <li><strong>Relations:</strong> Warm cooperation with elven envoys.</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~1200 B.F. — Rise of the Atlantean Empire</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Republic becomes empire under the Thalassirion Dynasty.</li>
  <li><strong>Power:</strong> Mastery of magic + technology leads to dominance.</li>
  <li><strong>Alliance:</strong> Treaties with elves begin the long Elven Project.</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~345–300 B.F. — Reign of King Damakeron</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Damakeron inherits an empire at its height.</li>
  <li><strong>Rule:</strong> Expands the Elven Project into vast, secret experiments.</li>
  <li><strong>Goal:</strong> Seek dominion over the leylines—“The Kraken.”</li>
  <li><strong>Outcome:</strong> His final act ruptures a leyline, triggering the Cataclysm.</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">0 A.F. — The Cataclysm</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Atlantis is annihilated in fire, storm, and upheaval.</li>
  <li><strong>Effect:</strong> Continents shift; cities drown.</li>
  <li><strong>Aftermath:</strong> Leylines destabilize; the Age of Empire ends.</li>
  <li><strong>Epoch:</strong> Beginning of the A.F. (After Fall) calendar.</li>
</ul>


<!-- AGE AFTER THE FALL -->
<h2 style="color:#f0dfc4; margin-top:2rem;">The Age After the Fall</h2>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">0–100 A.F. — The Century of Darkness</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> World plunges into disorder.</li>
  <li><strong>Condition:</strong> Seas rage; skies dim; civilizations crumble.</li>
  <li><strong>Power Vacuum:</strong> Warlords and remnant Atlanteans battle for fragments.</li>
  <li><strong>Cultural Memory:</strong> Atlantis slips into myth.</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~100 A.F. — Founding of Deutera Atlantis</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Survivors create the “Second Empire.”</li>
  <li><strong>Aim:</strong> Reclaim knowledge and restore order.</li>
  <li><strong>Character:</strong> A coalition of successor states.</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~500 A.F. — The Veiling of the Elves</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Elves withdraw permanently.</li>
  <li><strong>Action:</strong> The Veil is raised—realms vanish from human sight.</li>
  <li><strong>Reason:</strong> Protection from dwindling numbers and wounded leylines.</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~700 A.F. — The Arrival of the Picts</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Hyperborean tribes land in northern Neokosmos.</li>
  <li><strong>Culture:</strong> Maritime, fierce, tribal; they merge with locals.</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~1000 A.F. — Great Karn’s Conquest</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Pictish tribes unite under Great Karn.</li>
  <li><strong>Action:</strong> Sack Deutera Atlantis, ending the Second Empire.</li>
  <li><strong>Legacy:</strong> Continent fractures into tribes and small realms.</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~1030 A.F. — Death of Great Karn</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Karn dies under mysterious circumstances.</li>
  <li><strong>Result:</strong> Pictish unity dissolves.</li>
  <li><strong>Effect:</strong> Cultures diverge, shaping future kingdoms.</li>
</ul>


<!-- AGE OF FAITH AND EMPIRE -->
<h2 style="color:#f0dfc4; margin-top:2rem;">The Age of Faith and Empire</h2>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~1500 A.F. — Rise of the Ruestrian Empire</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Ruestria begins to form.</li>
  <li><strong>Character:</strong> Disciplined, bureaucratic, guided by the Cult of Sol Eternel.</li>
  <li><strong>Significance:</strong> Ends the long barbarian age.</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~1700 A.F. — The Kingdom of Alnhama</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Alnhama rises on the island chains.</li>
  <li><strong>Relations:</strong> Ally, then rival to Ruestria; famed for mercenary fleets.</li>
  <li><strong>Era:</strong> Trade expansion and rediscovery of ancient lore.</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~2000 A.F. — Founding of Fresnia</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Fresnia formed atop ruins of Deutera Atlantis.</li>
  <li><strong>Culture:</strong> Proud, independent, seekers of Atlantean secrets.</li>
</ul>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~2400 A.F. — The Alnhaman Revolution</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Royal family overthrown.</li>
  <li><strong>Outcome:</strong> Republic of Alnhama declared.</li>
  <li><strong>Impact:</strong> Cold civil war between royalists and republicans.</li>
</ul>


<!-- MODERN AGE -->
<h2 style="color:#f0dfc4; margin-top:2rem;">The Modern Age</h2>

<h3 style="color:#ead7b7; margin-top:1.2rem;">~2500 A.F. — The Present Day</h3>
<ul style="margin-left:1rem;">
  <li><strong>Event:</strong> Damakeron’s sword uncovered in a ruined Atlantean outpost.</li>
  <li><strong>Reaction:</strong> Fear and fervor spread across scholars and temples.</li>
  <li><strong>Meaning:</strong> First tangible proof of Old Atlantis in millennia.</li>
</ul>
